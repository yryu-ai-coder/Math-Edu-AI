ğŸ“ 1. Congruent Figures

Two figures are congruent if they have the same shape and size.

ğŸ” Congruent Triangles

Two triangles are congruent if their corresponding sides and angles are equal.

Congruence Criteria:

  • SSS (Side-Side-Side): All three sides are equal
  • SAS (Side-Angle-Side): Two sides and the included angle are equal
  • ASA (Angle-Side-Angle): Two angles and the included side are equal
  • AAS (Angle-Angle-Side): Two angles and a non-included side are equal
