Problem 1: Taxi Fare

A taxi has a base fare of $3,000 and charges $1,000 per kilometer.

Express the fare y dollars as a function of distance x kilometers.

Answer: y = 1000x + 3000

Explanation: Base fare $3000 plus $1000 per kilometer, so it's a linear function with slope 1000 and y-intercept 3000.

Problem 2: Water Tank Height

Water is filling a tank at a constant rate. After 1 minute the height is 2cm, after 3 minutes it's 6cm.

Express the water height h cm as a function of time t minutes.

Answer: h = 2t

Explanation: Height increases by 2cm per minute, so it's a linear function with slope 2 and y-intercept 0.

Problem 3: Population Growth

If a city's population increases by 3% annually, and the current population is 1 million,

express the population P after t years as a function.

Answer: P = 1,000,000 √ó (1.03)^t

Explanation: 3% annual growth means a growth rate of 1.03. This is an exponential function.
